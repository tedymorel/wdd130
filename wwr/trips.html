Featured Trips

Los Dientes del Diablo

Experience the ultimate thrill on the "Devil's Teeth" rapids. This advanced trip is perfect for experienced rafters seeking an adrenaline rush. Navigate challenging class IV-V rapids through stunning canyons.

  • Duration: 2 Days
  • Difficulty: Advanced (Class IV-V)
  • Includes: Gear, meals, experienced guides

Big Mallard

A fantastic intermediate trip offering a mix of exciting rapids and calm stretches. Ideal for families and groups looking for a memorable day on the water. Enjoy beautiful scenery and wildlife viewing opportunities.

  • Duration: 1 Day
  • Difficulty: Intermediate (Class III)
  • Includes: Gear, lunch, professional guides

Half Day Adventure

Perfect for those with limited time or first-time rafters. This shorter trip provides a taste of white water rafting fun without committing to a full day. Great for beginners and families with young children.

  • Duration: Half Day (3-4 hours)
  • Difficulty: Beginner (Class II)
  • Includes: Gear, light refreshments, friendly guides

Salmon River Run

Embark on a multi-day journey down the majestic Salmon River. This trip combines exhilarating rapids with serene wilderness camping. Discover hidden hot springs and abundant wildlife.

  • Duration: 3 Days / 2 Nights
  • Difficulty: Intermediate (Class III-IV)
  • Includes: All gear, gourmet meals, camping equipment, expert guides
